About Me

A passionate home baker turned tech enthusiast, now on an exciting journey to become a full-stack developer. My transition into the tech world was sparked by a deep curiosity for how digital experiences are built and a love for problem-solving. After taking several introductory courses, I discovered my passion for frontend development, and I am now actively expanding my skills to master backend technologies and eventually become a well-rounded full-stack developer.

Beyond coding, I am also an entrepreneur. I founded Thamani Jewellery, an eCommerce store that brings authentic African jewellery to customers in the USA and Europe. My business is deeply rooted in my Kenyan heritage, as I work closely with local artisans to showcase their craftsmanship on a global scale. This venture has given me hands-on experience in eCommerce, digital marketing, and web technologies, further fueling my interest in tech.

What excites me most about tech is its limitless potential to solve real-world problems and create meaningful experiences. I thrive in hands-on, practical learning environments, where I can build, experiment, and continuously improve. Whether it's developing a sleek, user-friendly interface or diving into the logic behind the scenes, I love every step of the creative process.
